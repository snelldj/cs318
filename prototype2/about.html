History

We are very proud of our history with the program, having qualified for Nationals for over ten years in a row now, not to mention Top 10 finishes in each of the last four years. We are a program that is open to new student athletes, but we proudly emphasize the competitiveness of the level of which we play.

This year, Nationals took place in Kansas City, MO, where we made the Elite Eight and took 5th in the nation for Division II. Last year, we made the Final Four and finished 3rd in the nation in Louisville, KY. Both of the previous two years before that, we took 9th in the nation both years, making it to the Sweet Sixteen both years, in Kansas City, MO and Reno, NV.

Many of our alumni continue their volleyball careers after graduation. Some of our previous players have gone on to coach for high school and college teams, while some others have taken their talents to the AVP Tour, where they play beach volleyball professionally.
